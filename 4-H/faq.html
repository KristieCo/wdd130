FAQ

  1. What is 4-H? 4-H is a non-formal educational program for young people.
  2. Who can join 4-H? Children can join 4-H when they are in kindergarten and are at least 5 years old.
  3. What are the projects? 4-H members can choose projects in areas like family living, environmental stewardship, and social sciences.
  4. Do I have to complete all my projects? No, 4-H members can choose to complete one or more projects each year.
  5. Is there a fee to join? Yes, there is an enrollment fee to join 4-H.
  6. What do the H's stand for? The H's stand for Head, Heart, Hands, and Health.
